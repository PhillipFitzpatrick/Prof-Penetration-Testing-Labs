## Complete the TryHackMe Labs 

I'm going to suggest several free labs you should complete on the tryHackMe platform. Again, you might want to make notes of anything useful that you find. I'll list a few additional labs you might want to try completing this week also.
___


### Essential Labs: 

1. [Beginner Path Intro](https://tryhackme.com/room/beginnerpathintro) - Get a short introduction to a few of the security topics you'll be learning about.

2. [Starting Out in cyber Sec](https://tryhackme.com/room/startingoutincybersec) - Learn about the different career paths in Cyber Security and how TryHackMe can help!

3. [Jr Security analyst](https://tryhackme.com/room/jrsecanalystintrouxo) - Play through a day in the life of a Junior Security Analyst, their responsibilities and qualifications needed to land a role as an analyst.

4. [Pentesting fundamentals](https://tryhackme.com/room/pentestingfundamentals) - Learn the important ethics and methodologies behind every pentest.

5. [Hacker Methodology](https://tryhackme.com/room/hackermethodology) - Introduction to the Hacker Methodology

6. [Red Team Engagements](https://tryhackme.com/room/redteamengagements) - Learn the steps and procedures of a red team engagement, including planning, frameworks, and documentation.

7. [Defensive Security](https://tryhackme.com/room/defensivesecurityhq) - Introducing defensive security and related topics, such as threat intelligence, SOC, DFIR, and SIEM.

8. [Tutorial](https://tryhackme.com/room/tutorial) - Learn how to use a TryHackMe room to start your upskilling in cyber security.. 


### Essential Labs: 

1. [Security Awareness Intro](https://tryhackme.com/room/securityawarenessintro) - An introduction to security awareness; why its important, the impact of being attacked, different threat actors and basic account security.
